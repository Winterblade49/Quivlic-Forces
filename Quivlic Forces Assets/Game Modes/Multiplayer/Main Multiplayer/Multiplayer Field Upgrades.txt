Munitions Box: Deploy a box of ammo, weapons, and equipment for you and your teammates.

Recon Drone: Pilot a small recon drone that marks enemies and equipment.

Trophy System: Deployable autonomous defense system that destroys up to three incoming enemy projectiles.

Deployable Cover: Portable, instant cover.

Dead Silence: Temporarily makes your footsteps silent.

Stopping Power Rounds: Reload to equip stopping power rounds that deal extra damage to vehicles and killstreaks.

EMP Drone: A drone that emits a burst of electromagnetic energy, disabling enemy electronics.

Weapon Drop: Drop a cache of weapons for you and your teammates to use.
